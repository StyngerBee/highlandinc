About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Highland Consulting, LLC, your one-stop destination for comprehensive consulting services tailored to meet your unique business needs. Founded by a seasoned professional with a rich tapestry of experiences, Highland Consulting is deeply rooted in a tradition of excellence, hard work, and innovation.

Our Founder's Journey

Born in Scotland in 1951 and raised on Long Island, NY, our founder comes from humble beginnings. The child of a baker and a homemaker, he embarked on a transformative educational journey, earning a Bachelor of Arts in Education from Dowling College in 1973 and a Master of Science in Finance from Adelphi University in 1976.

Professional Background

His career commenced as an auditor at Hurdman & Cranston, now part of Price Waterhouse Coopers, and he passed the CPA exam in New York in 1981. Subsequently, he delved into the corporate world, starting as a Staff Accountant at Brinkmann Instruments, Inc. and rising to the position of Controller over a span of 13 years. A move to St. Thomas in 1994 marked a new chapter, one filled with challenges and opportunities alike, such as weathering Hurricane Marilyn. Over the years, he's held pivotal roles in numerous companies, including serving as Controller at Colombian Emeralds for 11 years and ascending from CFO to CEO at Choice Communications. He's also contributed his financial expertise to Heavy Materials and aided several local companies in securing Economic Development Commission (EDC) benefits.
