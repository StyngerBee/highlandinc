About Us

Welcome to Highland Consulting, LLC, your one-stop destination for comprehensive consulting services tailored to meet your unique business needs. Founded by a seasoned professional with a rich tapestry of experiences, Highland Consulting is deeply rooted in a tradition of excellence, hard work, and innovation.

David J. Haddow
Born in Scotland and raised on Long Island, NY, Dave earned a Bachelor of Arts in Education from Dowling College, NY in 1973 and a Master of Science in Finance from Adelphi University, NY in 1976. His career commenced as an auditor at Hurdman & Cranston, now part of Price Waterhouse Coopers, and he passed the CPA exam in New York in 1981. Subsequently, he delved into the corporate world, starting as a Staff Accountant at Brinkmann Instruments, Inc. and rising to the position of Controller over a span of 13 years.

A move to St. Thomas in 1994 marked a new chapter, one filled with challenges and opportunities alike, such as weathering Hurricanes Marilyn, Irma and Maria. Over the years, he's held pivotal roles in numerous companies on St. Thomas including:

  • Cardow as Controller
  • Colombian Emeralds as Controller
  • Heavy Materials as Controller
  • Choice Communications - ascending from CFO to CEO
  • He's also aided several local companies in securing Economic Development Commission (EDC) benefits


    • Over the years, Dave has bought/sold real estate, owned rental properties; owned restaurants and a waterfront bar in St. Thomas, and served as a college professor on Long Island for 11 semesters. Currently a Deacon in the St. Thomas Reformed Church, he enjoys hobbies like writing, reading, cooking, traveling and maintaining an active lifestyle.
    Rosh D. Alger
    Born in Algeria, Rosh earned a Bachelor of Science in Finance & International Business in 1991 followed by an MBA in 1997 – both from Woodbury University in California. He then received his J. D in 2002 from the Syracuse University College of Law. That was followed by an LLM in Taxation from the University of Florida Levin College of Law in 2003.

    Rosh was admitted to the New York State Bar in 2002 and the US Virgin Islands Bar in 2004. He has also been admitted to practice in the Supreme Court of the State of New York; the Superior Court of the US Virgin Islands and the District Court of the US Virgin Islands.

    Prior to his practice of law, Rosh gained experience working in various positions including:

  • Sumitomo Bank, Los Angeles, CA - as a Systems Analyst
  • PSCANN, Inc., Los Angeles, CA – as an Administrator
  • ARYA, LP, Santa Monica, CA – as an Assistant Vice President


    • Rosh relocated to St. Thomas in 2003 where he joined the law firm of Tom Bolt & Associates. In 2008, he founded the Alger Law Office and is also Chairman of the Corporate & Tax Planning Group. In 2016 Rosh founded Highland Consulting, LLC which offers a wide range of other professional services.
